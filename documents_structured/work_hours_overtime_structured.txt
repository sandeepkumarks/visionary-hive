Section 1: Standard Work Hours
The standard work week is 40 hours, from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Employees are expected to be punctual and attend work during these hours.

Section 2: Flexible Work Hours
Employees may apply for flexible work hours. Approval is subject to the discretion of their manager and team needs.

Section 3: Overtime Policy
Non-exempt employees are eligible for overtime pay at 1.5 times their hourly rate for hours worked beyond 40 per week.

Section 4: Weekend and Holiday Work
Employees required to work on weekends or public holidays are entitled to compensatory time off or additional pay, as outlined by company policy.
