Section 1: Eligibility for Remote Work
Employees in roles that do not require physical presence at the office may apply for remote work. Remote work eligibility is determined by department and role.

Section 2: Equipment and Tools
The company provides necessary equipment for remote work, including laptops and secure access to the company network.

Section 3: Remote Work Hours
Remote employees are expected to adhere to the same work hours as in-office employees unless approved otherwise by their manager.

Section 4: Communication and Reporting
Remote employees must be available for virtual meetings and regular check-ins with their teams. Daily or weekly reports may be required.
