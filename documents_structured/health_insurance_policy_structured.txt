Section 1: Health Insurance Coverage
The company provides health insurance coverage for all full-time employees, covering medical, dental, and vision care. This policy also extends to dependents, including spouses and children.

Section 2: Health Insurance Enrollment
Employees must enroll in the health insurance program within 30 days of joining the company or during the annual open enrollment period.

Section 3: Dental and Vision Insurance
The health plan includes both dental and vision insurance, which covers routine exams, cleanings, and basic optical services.
