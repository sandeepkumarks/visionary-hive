Section 1: Learning and Development Programs
The company offers various learning programs, including leadership training, technical skills workshops, and soft skills development.

Section 2: Professional Development Budget
Employees have an annual budget of $1,500 for professional development. This can be used for courses, certifications, and conferences.

Section 3: Tuition Assistance
Employees pursuing higher education relevant to their job roles may apply for tuition assistance. Reimbursement is subject to approval and successful completion of courses.

Section 4: Mentorship Program
New employees are assigned mentors to help them integrate into the company. Mentorship programs last for the first 6 months of employment.

Section 5: External Conferences
Employees are encouraged to attend industry conferences. Requests for conference attendance must be approved by their manager and HR.
