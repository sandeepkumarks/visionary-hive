Section 1: Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 15 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave must be scheduled and approved by the employee's manager in advance.

Section 2: Sick Leave
Employees may take up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. For absences exceeding 3 days, a medical certificate is required.

Section 3: Parental Leave
Eligible employees can take up to 12 weeks of parental leave. This leave is unpaid unless specified by additional state or federal policies.

Section 4: Bereavement Leave
In the event of the death of an immediate family member, employees are entitled to 5 days of paid bereavement leave.

Section 5: Carry Over Policy
Unused annual leave cannot be carried over to the following year, except in exceptional cases approved by HR.
